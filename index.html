Whak-A-Mole

Game Instructions

Welcome to the Whack-a-Mole Game!

there are 4 types of moles

Regular Mole: Worth 5 points. Click to whack it.

Strong Mole: Worth 10 points, but requires 3 hits to defeat.

Evil Mole: Decreases your time by 5 seconds if clicked.

Freezing Mole: Pauses the game for 3 seconds if clicked.

Have Fun !!
